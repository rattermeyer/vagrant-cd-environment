The Continuous Delivery Playground

Welcome, stranger! You have successfully set-up your continuous delivery playground. The links below will take you to the most important components of this playground.

CD Tool
Automate and streamline the build-test-release cycle for worry-free, continuous delivery of your product.

Service Discovery
Because of the dynamic nature of a clould based environment a service discovery and configuration solution should be in place. Consul is: Service discovery and configuration made easy. Distributed, highly-available and data-center aware.

Container Registry
Docker Registry is an open source application dedicated to the storage and distribution of your Docker images. Its seamless architecture allows both for fine grain integration with other systems and high-level scalability. Aggressively developed, its vibrant community includes industry leaders and users using it at the core of their images distribution solutions.

Log Management
Graylog is a fully integrated open source log management platform for collecting, indexing, and analyzing both structured and unstructured data from almost any source.

Binary Artifact Management
Nexus manages software components required for development, deployment, and provisioning. What a version control system is to source code, a binary repository is the same for the results of your builds.
